About Me

I am a first-year Ph.D. student at Nanjing University (NJU), supervised by Prof. Yao Yao, Prof. Xun Cao and Prof. Tieniu Tan. Previously, I pursued a Ph.D. at the Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT). I earned my M.S. from the Harbin Engineering University (HEU) in 2021, where I was advised by Prof. Jian Guan.

I worked at leading 3D generation startups DreamTech (Neural4D), VAST (Tripo AI) and Taichi (Meshy AI), where I contributed to their early 3D generation breakthroughs.
