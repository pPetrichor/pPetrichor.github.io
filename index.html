About Me

Nice to meet you! I am a first-year Ph.D. student at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), supervised by Prof. Qifeng Chen. I earned my M.S. from the Nanjing University (NJU) in 2025, where I was advised by Prof. Limin Wang. I obtained my B.S. degree in Computer Science from Nanjing University in 2022. My research is supported by the prestigious Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme.

My research interests lie in video generation and world model.
